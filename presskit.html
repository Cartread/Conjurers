Description

Card + Tactics game based on creatures of the Zodiac and their powerful combinations.

Features

  • Innovative blend of Cards and Terrain Tactics.
  • Complete Zodiac of 60 Animated Creatures and Hybrids.
  • 150 Cards and Deck Builder.
  • Challenging computer opponent; helpful tooltips and tutorial.

Gameplay Details

Single-player campaign:
Choose a Conjurer, starter Deck and Domain.
Select a neighboring Domain to battle, along with a few Creatures to attack with.
Picking Creatures which move well on the battlefield's terrain is smart.

Each player starts with three Cards drawn from their custom Deck and draws another each turn.
Cards add a layer of strategy which makes this game unique.
Select Ability cards which best suit your Creatures and Spells which counter your opponent.

Creatures cost Mana to "activate" each turn, same as Cards, so players must choose carefully how they spend their Mana. When Creatures "activate," they add their surroundings to your Domain and may acquire Mana Towers or other useful objects. Then you may move and attack with that Creature.

Each player has their own Tower (safely in the back of their Domain) and a unique power to defend it. Destroying your opponent's Tower is how you win.
After winning a battle, you may collect any Creature on the field for future battles, spend EXP on new Cards and Creatures and expand your campaign Domain.

Two of three boss battles complete.

Developer's History

My inspiration for indie game development began with "Mount & Blade" (2008), which I have made many mods for ("Black Knight", "Hero & Blade", "Peasants") and am proudly in their community credits.
"Conjurers" is my first commercial game that is feature complete and ready for players!
