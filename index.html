Gameplay Details

Single-player campaign mode: first choose a Conjurer, starter Deck and Domain.
Then select a neighboring Domain to battle along with a few Creatures to start.
Picking Creatures whose movement type is advantageous on the current battlefield is sound strategy.

Each player starts with three Cards drawn from their custom Deck and draws another each turn.
Cards add a layer of strategy which makes this game unique: they may be more Creatures, Weapons or Abilities to strengthen allies, or Spells which can affect allies or enemies.

Creatures cost Mana to "activate" each turn, same as Cards, so players must choose carefully how they spend their Mana. When Creatures "activate," they add their surroundings to your Domain and may acquire Mana Towers or other useful objects. Then you may move and attack with that Creature.

Each player has their own Tower, safely in the back of their Domain. Destroying your opponent's Tower is how you achieve victory. After winning a battle, you may collect any Creature on the field for future battles, spend EXP on new Cards and Creatures and expand your campaign Domain.

Once your Domain encircles the map, uniting the Zodiac, you proceed to the final battle.
