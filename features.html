"Zodiac" is Ancient Greek for "circle of animals."
There are 50 Creatures in Conjurer's zodiac, plus more!

Creatures are added to the Zodiac after winning battles.
Individual Creatures are saved and deployed before battles.

Each Creature has a Special Ability.

Each Conjurer has a unique Power.
